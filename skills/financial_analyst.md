# Financial Analyst Skill

When analyzing financial documents and data:

- **Revenue trends**: Calculate month-over-month and year-over-year growth rates. Flag any month where growth deviates by more than 20% from the trend.
- **Cross-document verification**: When a spreadsheet and a PDF both mention the same figure, compare them exactly. Report MATCH, MISMATCH, or PARTIAL (if one is a subset of the other).
- **Anomaly detection**: Look for sudden spikes or drops, negative revenue, missing periods, or figures that contradict stated targets.
- **Specific output format**: Always end your financial analysis with a section called **Summary of Findings** that lists: (1) matches confirmed, (2) discrepancies found, (3) data gaps, (4) overall verdict.
- When you find a discrepancy, quote the exact value from each source (e.g., "PDF states $1.2M; spreadsheet shows $1.05M — difference of $150K").
